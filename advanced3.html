FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know before joining

How exactly do I earn money?

You earn Ksh.100 for every successful share of our hotel promotions. When someone engages with your shared link (clicks, views, or books), you get credited. The more you share, the more you earn! We provide you with all the marketing materials and your unique tracking link.

When and how do I get paid?

Payments are processed every Friday afternoon directly to your M-Pesa account. You'll receive a confirmation message once payment is sent. Minimum payout is Ksh.500, but most affiliates earn much more than this each week.

Is there any registration fee?

Yes, there's a small one-time account setup fee (Ksh.300 for 100 shares or Ksh.500 for 200 shares account). This covers your unique tracking link, administrative costs, and ensures only serious affiliates join the program. Many affiliates recover this cost in their first day of sharing.

How much time do I need to invest?

You can work as little or as much as you want. Many affiliates spend 1-2 hours daily sharing links on social media and earn Ksh.5,000-10,000 weekly. The more time you invest, the more you can earn. It's perfect as a side hustle or even full-time income.

What if I don't have many social media followers?

No problem! You can share in WhatsApp groups (family, friends, community), post on Facebook marketplace, or even share directly with contacts. Many successful affiliates started with small networks. We'll also teach you strategies to maximize your shares regardless of your current following.

Is there any training provided?

Yes! After registration, you'll receive our complete affiliate guide with proven sharing strategies, best times to post, and templates you can use. We also have a dedicated WhatsApp support group where top affiliates share tips daily.

Can I really make this a full-time income?

Absolutely! Our top affiliates earn Ksh.50,000+ monthly by treating this as a serious business. The key is consistency and applying our proven strategies. Many have quit their regular jobs to do this full-time because the earning potential is unlimited.
